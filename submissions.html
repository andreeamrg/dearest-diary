Submissions


Terms and conditions for film submissions.

Contact us by email at dearestdiaryenquiries@googlemail.com if you do not understand any of the terms and conditions.

You must not post any videos for the purpose of advertising or promote your own products and services without prior agreement. You may only do so if you have been granted permission after contacting us at dearestdiaryenquiries@googlemail.com

You must not submit videos containing gross violence, offensive language, illegal content, drug and alcohol misuse, pornography or sexually explicit content.

Be sensitive in the making of your film, do not create content that could be deemed as triggering or offensive to the audience.

You are urged to seek professional medical advice in regards to individual situations. You must not rely solely on the content posted on this website.

If you wish to remove your website at any time contact us by email at dearestdiaryenquiries@googlemail.com

We reserve the right to remove, modify or move posts at our discretion without explanation.

Films breaching these rules will be be denied by moderators and will not be added onto the website.

How to submit:

Submit your film by email. Contact: dearestdiarysubmissions@googlemail.com

    1. Email us your film in MP4 format. The film can be up to 5 minutes in length.
    Include the following information in the email:

    Film name
    Written poem or text (if applicable)
    The music source in your film (if applicable)

    Be sure to check the copyright restrictions of music included in your film.

    2. You will receive an email back either accepting or denying your film submission.

    3. If accepted, your film and poem / text will appear on the website within 5 working days.
